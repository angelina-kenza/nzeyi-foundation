Introduction

Our foundation at a glance

Who we are

Dr. Amos Nzeyi Foundation is a non-profit organization providing sponsorship opportunities to disadvantaged students with high academic performance in Kigezi region, Uganda.

Vision

All high-performaning students in Kigezi region have access to high quality education, regardless of background.

Mission

Identify students from disadvantaged backgrounds with high academic performance in Kigezi region and avail them access to continuous education.

About

Our journey so far.

  • 2018

    Foundation establishment

    The Dr. Amos Nzeyi Foundation is established in Uganda. It receives seed funding from members of the Nzeyi family.

  • 2018-2019

    First batch of students

    After careful consideration, the first batch of high-performing and underpriviliged students from Kigezi is entered into the program.

  • 2020

    COVID pandemic

    With lockdowns across the country, the Nzeyi Foundation scales up its efforts to provide vital assistance to our students' families.

  • 2024

    Bearing fruit

    The first students from our early cohorts are at the top of their class.

We strive to promote welfare for the family of the beneficiaries whenever there is a need. E.g. during the COVID lockdowns we provided them with all food items and material to continue their studies.

Shallote Naume Ahurira

3rd child out of 5, in a peasant family.

Shallote was taken on by the Foundation in 2018 when she was in primary 6 (Six).
By this time she was almost dropping out of school; her peasant guardian family (a single mother) was not in a position to support her education.
Shallote is now in Senior 4 (Four) at Kigezi High School.

Ariho Emmanuel

7th born, in a peasant family.

Ariho's peasant family were not able to support his education.
None of his elder siblings has studied beyond Senior Four.
Ariho is a well behaved, polite and consistently hard-working learner. He has been consistently at the top of his class since the Foundation took him on.

Apollo Alex Niwabeine

1st born, in a family of farmers.

Apollo Alex was taken on by the foundation in 2018 when he was in primary level.
By that time he was almost giving up on education, as it was not easy for the parents to cover his school fees and sustain their family.
Apollo is now in Senior 5 at Kigezi High School and very settled in his learning.
He is a humble, well disciplined and hard working young man.
